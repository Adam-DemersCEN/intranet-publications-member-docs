# Editing a Publication

You can update the information of an existing publication at any time.

## Accessing the edit form

There are two ways to access the edit form.

**From the publication record** — open the detail record by clicking on the publication title, then click the **Edit** button.

<figure markdown>
  ![Edit button on the record](../assets/screenshots/edit-button-detail.png)
  <figcaption>Click "Edit" from the publication record</figcaption>
</figure>

**From the list** — click the publication's menu in the list, then select **Edit**.

<figure markdown>
  ![Edit button in the list](../assets/screenshots/edit-button-list.png)
  <figcaption>Click "Edit" from the list menu</figcaption>
</figure>

## Updating the information

The edit form is identical to the creation form. Make the desired corrections or additions in the relevant fields.

<figure markdown>
  ![Edit form](../assets/screenshots/edit-form.png)
  <figcaption>Publication edit form</figcaption>
</figure>

## Saving changes

Once your changes are complete, click **Save** to update the publication.

<figure markdown>
  ![Save changes button](../assets/screenshots/edit-save.png)
  <figcaption>Click "Save" to confirm your changes</figcaption>
</figure>
