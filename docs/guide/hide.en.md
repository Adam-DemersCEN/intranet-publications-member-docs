# Hiding a Publication

You can hide a publication so it no longer appears in your personal list. Note that the publication is not deleted — it is only hidden from your list and will remain visible in the CEN catalogue. If you actually want to delete a publication, contact an administrator who can do it for you.

## Accessing the hide option

There are two ways to hide a publication.

**From the publication record** — open the detail record by clicking on the publication title, then click the **Delete** button.

<figure markdown>
  ![Delete button on the record](../assets/screenshots/hide-button-detail.png)
  <figcaption>Click "Delete" from the publication record</figcaption>
</figure>

**From the list** — click the publication's menu in the list, then select **Delete**.

<figure markdown>
  ![Delete button in the list](../assets/screenshots/hide-button-list.png)
  <figcaption>Click "Delete" from the list menu</figcaption>
</figure>

## Confirming the action

You will be redirected to a confirmation page. Click **Hide publication** to hide the publication, or **Cancel** to go back.

<figure markdown>
  ![Hide confirmation page](../assets/screenshots/hide-confirm.png)
  <figcaption>Confirm hiding the publication</figcaption>
</figure>

Once hidden, the publication will no longer appear in your personal list, but will remain visible in the CEN catalogue.

## Making a publication visible again

To make a hidden publication visible again in your list, contact an administrator who can restore it for you.
