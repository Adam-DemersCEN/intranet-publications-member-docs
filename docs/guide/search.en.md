# Searching Publications

The publications module lets you browse and filter all publications by CEN members.

## Accessing the publications list

From the module home page, publications are displayed as a list. You can scroll through the list or use the filtering tools to narrow down your results.

<figure markdown>
  ![Publications list](../assets/screenshots/search-list.png)
  <figcaption>List of member publications</figcaption>
</figure>

## Filtering publications

Several filters are available to refine your results:

- **Author** — filter by a CEN member's name
- **Year** — limit results to one or more publication years
- **Publication type** — article, book, chapter, conference proceedings, etc.

Select one or more filters, then apply them to update the list.

<figure markdown>
  ![Search filters](../assets/screenshots/search-filters.png)
  <figcaption>Publications filter panel</figcaption>
</figure>

## Viewing a publication

Click on a publication title to open its full record: authors, abstract, DOI, year, type, and any other available metadata.

  ![Publication detail](../assets/screenshots/search-detail.png)
  <figcaption>Detailed publication record</figcaption>
</figure>
