# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**I don't have access to the intranet. What should I do?**

Contact a CEN administrator to get an account. This tutorial is intended for members who already have active access.

---

**I can't see the publications module in the intranet. What should I do?**

Make sure you are logged in with your CEN account. If the module still does not appear, contact an administrator to verify your permissions.

---

## Creating and editing publications

**Which fields are required when creating a publication?**

Required fields vary depending on the publication type and are identified by an asterisk. Step 3 of the creation and edit form contains fields specific to the publication type selected in step 1.

---

**Can I edit a publication after creating it?**

Yes. You can edit a publication at any time from its detail record or from the list menu. See the [Edit a publication](guide/edit.md) guide.

---

## Importing publications

**Which reference managers can export BibTeX files?**

Most popular reference managers support it, including Zotero, Mendeley, EndNote, and JabRef.

---

**My BibTeX import failed. What should I do?**

Check that your `.bib` file is valid and correctly formatted. Special characters or incorrect encoding can cause errors. Try re-exporting the file from your reference manager.

---

**Can I import from ORCID more than once?**

Yes. The ORCID import detects publications already in your list and only imports new entries.

---

## Exporting publications

**Which export format should I choose?**

- Choose **BibTeX** if you want to import your publications into a reference manager.
- Choose **CSV** or **Excel (XLSX)** if you want to work with your data in a spreadsheet.

---

**Does the export include all my publications or only the ones currently displayed?**

The bulk export respects any active filters at the time of export. If no filters are active, all your publications are exported.

---

## Hiding and deleting publications

**What is the difference between hiding and deleting a publication?**

Hiding a publication removes it from your personal list, but it remains visible in the CEN catalogue. Deletion is permanent and must be performed by an administrator.

---

**How do I permanently delete a publication?**

Contact a CEN administrator. You cannot delete a publication yourself from within the module.

---

**How do I recover a hidden publication?**

Contact a CEN administrator who can make it visible again in your list.
